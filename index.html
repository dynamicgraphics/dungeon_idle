⚔ HOW TO PLAY

👆
Click the MonsterTap the monster in the battle screen to deal instant bonus damage. Great for killing tough bosses!
⚔
Buy Heroes (auto-attack)Heroes fight automatically. Each costs more than the last but deals far more damage. Unlock all 8 types!
⬆
Upgrades (multipliers)Once you own enough heroes, buy milestone upgrades. Each doubles or more that hero's damage permanently.
🏰
Floor ProgressionEvery monster killed advances you one floor. Harder monsters = more gold. Every 10th floor is a BOSS!
✨
Prestige (once you hit floor 20+)Reset your run for 💎 Gems. Gems give a permanent gold bonus to all future runs — snowballs fast!
💾
Auto-saveGame saves every 30 seconds and earns offline gold for up to 8 hours while you're away.
